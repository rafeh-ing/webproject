Adoption FAQs

What are your adoption fees?

Our adoption fees vary by animal and typically range from 50dt-200dt. This includes spay/neuter, vaccinations, microchipping, and a health check.

What requirements do you have for adopters?

Adopters must be at least 21 years old, provide proof of address, and demonstrate the ability to care for the animal.

Can I return a pet if it doesn't work out?

Yes, we have a lifetime return policy. If for any reason you can no longer care for your adopted pet, we require that you return them to us.
